Company Search

Web service for searching details about companies, presenting graphs about incomes and employees. Enables user to compare companies, find related ones and read business articles connected.

Design Problems

  • Two different search engines - for companies and employees.
  • Interesting search results displayed at the bottom.
  • Desired information difficult to find.
  • Users visit company pages directly (traffic from google), they open only one page in service.

Goals

  • Search engine easier to use and scan. Results easier to find.
  • Promote the most popular search results.
  • Improve number of visited pages and time visitors spend on them.
  • Promote "Company Search" in Google results.
