Liftme

Web application which connects colleagues from one company to drive together from work. Passengers can search for drivers, set the route and time. Drivers can also add their way from work and offer a lift.


Goals

  • Build MVP product.
  • Find the easiest way to connect drivers and passengers from work.
  • Visualize route on the map.
  • Make a list of possible alternative drivers.
